Child Mortality

Which factors are most strongly associated with child mortality rates across different countries?

The death of a child is a tragedy no matter how it happened. However, there are still places on earth, where this is not an uncommon occurrence.
Understanding what causes a high child mortality rate would help in being able to allocate resources in the most effective ways to utilize their full potential and help in mitigating this widespread issue across the globe.

The main question in our chosen visualizations is to showcase which efforts and spending are the biggest contributors in combating child mortality rate in multitudes of varying countries across multiple continents.
